About Us

Our co-founders Harrison and Lucy have had a passion for food for as long as they could eat. They have been cooking together for 7 years, cooking for friends and family. They then began catering for parties and events and LOVE IT. Cooking a mix a flavours from around the world we love to open peoples minds to different tastes while providing them with old classics - with a twist!
